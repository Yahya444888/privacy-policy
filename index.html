Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the mobile application "Embassy of Lebanon - Riyadh". This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information that you provide while using our app.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the app. The app is informational and does not require users to input or submit any personal data.

Permissions

The app does not request or access any device permissions such as location, camera, microphone, or storage.

Data Sharing

No user data is collected, tracked, or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future to reflect any changes in the app or legal requirements. Users are encouraged to check this page regularly to stay informed.

Contact
